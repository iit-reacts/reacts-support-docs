title: How do I change my Reacts password?
---
You can change your password from inside Reacts by going to Settings --> My Account and choose option "Change password":

![Reset_password_EN.png](@assets/attachments/Reset_password_EN.png)

Password reset instructions will be send to your e-mail.

Another option to change your password is to go to the login page of our website, click on the forgot password button, enter the email address you used to create your account and we'll send you instructions to change your password.
