 **On the Menu bar you have following options:**

*    **Take a snapshot or record small video clip:**

**Watch out video tutorial on [Snapshots (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E15v33KpYJ8)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E15v33KpYJ8)**

![Snapshots_EN.png](@assets/attachments/Snapshots_EN.png)

Allows you to take a picture or video of whatever on a canvas. All the snapshots taken in a session appear in _**Snapshot Gallery**_ and are stored in Snapshot Library in a folders named after the date when a snapshot was taken.

*    **Manage your Layers:**

**Watch our video tutorial on [Layers (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s4FDiupsMiY)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s4FDiupsMiY)**

![](@assets/attachments/layers.png) 

If you have multiple _**Layered assets**_ on one scene, _**Layers menu**_ will help you to manage those assets

 ![](@assets/attachments/2014-10-16_12-05-44.png)

*   **Lock or unlock your background:**

**Watch out video tutorial on [Background (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstTGA0O\_EU)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hstTGA0O_EU)**

**![](@assets/attachments/2014-10-16_12-27-40.png)**

You can put object or input on canvas as a _**Background Asset**_ or as a _**Layered Asset**_.

To easily switch between managing _**Background**_ and _**Layered Assets**_ you can _**lock /unlock**_ your background

*   **Change camera ratio:**

**Watch out video tutorial on [Adjusting the format of the scene window (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rk9HVWj0vF0)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rk9HVWj0vF0)**

By default ratio is set to "Auto (best fit)

![](@assets/attachments/2014-10-16_12-26-09.png)

 ![](@assets/attachments/contacts8.png) _Please note Ratio need to be changed before adding any assets otherwise all assets will be removed._
