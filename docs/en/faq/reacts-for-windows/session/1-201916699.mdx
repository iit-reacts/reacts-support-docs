From assets panel you can import, organize and sync your assets (images, videos, 3D objects, snapshots, documents, checklists and reports), manage scenes in sessions.

#### **Watch video tutorial[Session: Assets (https://youtu.be/nd2WkCjcpxc)](https://youtu.be/nd2WkCjcpxc)**

First 3 options on the panel allow you to work with images, videos and 3D objects:

Snapshots that were taken during a session will be saved in _**Snapshot gallery**_under the folder corresponding to a date and time when a snapshot was taken
