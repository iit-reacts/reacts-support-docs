title: How do I change pointer setting?
---
To change pointer settings you need to be in a **Session interface.** From **Menu bar** at the top of a **Scene window** choose **Pointer setting** from Pointer dropdown:

![](@assets/attachments/2014-10-17_10-46-05.png)

![](@assets/attachments/2014-10-17_10-37-45.png)

 From **Pointer settings** window you can adjust pointer size, choose color:

![](@assets/attachments/2014-10-17_10-43-58.png)
