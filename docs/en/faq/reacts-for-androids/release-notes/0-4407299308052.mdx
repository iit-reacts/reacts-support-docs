**New features during a call:**  
- Call in Chat: quick access to a chat group with all participants of the call (closing the call closes the conversation)
- New way to access your conversations: quickly send a private message to a participant through the participant list
- Remove a participant during a call: The owner of the call can remove participants through the participant list

Bug fixes and minor improvements.
