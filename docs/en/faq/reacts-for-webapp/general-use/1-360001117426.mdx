**Reacts WebApp can ONLY be accessed directly from your browser using Chrome.**

Here are the available features of Reacts Web and a quick look at what will be available soon.

  
**Features Available**

*   Contact Management (add, remove, accept contact request)
*   Face to Face session
*   Receive screen sharing
*   Receive multiple video streams
*   Device selection
*   Send multiple video streams
*   Multiple Web Camera support
*   Instant messaging
*   Mute Web Camera
*   Share display
