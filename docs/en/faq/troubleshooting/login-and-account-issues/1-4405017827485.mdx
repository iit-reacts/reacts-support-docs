**In order to reset the password of a Reacts account**

Follow these few steps:

1\. Go to the login page of [Reacts.com](https://identity.iitreacts.com/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2Fconnect%2Fauthorize%2Fcallback%3Fclient_id%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.iitreacts.com%26response_type%3Dcode%2520id_token%26scope%3Dopenid%2520profile%2520https%253A%252F%252Fwww.iitreacts.com%26redirect_uri%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Freacts.com%252Foauth%252FExternalLoginCallback%26nonce%3Dedffffed7b8ff1fbe8613ca9c37f7526da2336b495d9b7362819666901aa97fe%26response_mode%3Dform_post%26ui_locales%3Dfr)

2\. Click on "Forgot your password"

3\. Enter the email address used to register to your Reacts account

4\. Click on "Send"

5\. Wait a few minutes and open your mailbox. You should have received the following email :

![mceclip0.png](@assets/attachments/mceclip0.png)

In this email:

1\. Click on "reset your password"

2\. Enter your new password (twice)

3\. Now, in order to log in to Reacts, this new password needs to be used.
