title: How to cancel my subscription
---
Reacts subscriptions are not auto-renewed. Therefore, the subscription will expire at the end of it's valid period and you will not be charged.

**If you still want to cancel your subscription:**

1- Be aware that no refund will be done

2- Submit your cancellation request by contacting Philips support
