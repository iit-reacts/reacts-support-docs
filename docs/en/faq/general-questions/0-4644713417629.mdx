title: How to receive my activation email again?
---
If you need to receive your activation email, please follow the steps below: 

\- go to https://reacts.com/

\- click on the log in button

\- at the bottom of the page, click on "Resend activation email"

![mceclip0.png](@assets/attachments/mceclip0.png)

\- enter your email address 

\- click on "Resend activation email"
