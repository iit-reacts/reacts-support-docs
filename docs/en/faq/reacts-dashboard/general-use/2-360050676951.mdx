OVERVIEW
--------

As an administrator you can distribute unassigned subscriptions from one Organizational Unit to another.

**\*Note this tutorial only to move unassigned subscriptions\***

**\*If you wish to move users with their assigned subscriptions, please refer to the tutorial : how to move yours users for from one org. unit to another**

PREREQUISITES
-------------

*   You must be an administrator of at least two Organization Units and have unassigned subscriptions available.
*   You must have access to the Reacts dashboard

DISTRIBUTING SUBSCRIPTIONS
--------------------------

### 1\. Click on "Distribute Subscriptions"

![distribute01-en.png](@assets/attachments/distribute01-en.png)

You will see:  
- The source organization unit: the unit that holds the subscriptions that you are going to use. This Organizational Unit can be changed by clicking on its name.
- The number of unassigned subscriptions available in this unit

### ![distribute02-en.png](@assets/attachments/distribute02-en.png)

###   
2\. Click on "Select a Destination Organization Unit"

### ![distribute03-en.png](@assets/attachments/distribute03-en.png)

###   
3\. Select the Organization Unit you want to transfer your subscriptions to.

### 4\. Click on "Select this Organization Unit"

![distribute04-en.png](@assets/attachments/distribute04-en.png)

### 5\. Indicate the number of PRO and/or Standard Subscriptions you want to transfer.

### 6\. Click on "Apply"

![distribute05-en.png](@assets/attachments/distribute05-en.png)

  
**Note:** you can transfer subscriptions to several different Organization Units at the same time by clicking on the "+" button located at the bottom left:

![distribute06-en.png](@assets/attachments/distribute06-en.png)
